The Best Movies According to Zeina

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

A Man Called Otto

This movie depicts a man named Otto's struggle to continue his life after the sorrowed death of his beloved one

All The Bright Places

This movie attempts to portrait an atypical love story between Finch who is a bipolar individual and Voilet who has recently experienced traumatic events

Bridge To Terabithia

This movie is teenage targeted with a beautiful flow of events and a surprising odd ending
